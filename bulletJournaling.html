Bullet Journaling for Students



  • Less Distraction - Using the help of electronic devices to keep track of things like your schedule or things you need to do is all well and good. But sometimes the temptation to hop onto social media apps or games could grip you. Something like this could never happen with a bullet journal since it is on paper.
  • Writing Things Helps You Remember Them - Sometimes just the act of writing things down is an effective way of branding it into your memory. The things you write down could be to-do lists, important reading lists etc. All of which would be beneficial for you to remember.
  • More Organized - A bullet journal might also inspire you to be more organized with not only your schedule for school, but for other things like daily water intake or workouts.
  • More Productive - Bullet journals are also a record of all the things you want and need to be accomplished. This is an easy way to keep track of them throughout your years as a student in a really organized way.
  • Become More Self Aware - Bullet journals can also serve as trackers for habits such as studying for at least 20 mins a day, doing your daily reading, and water intake. It can help you manage your life and provide a reflection of what you do in your day to day life.
