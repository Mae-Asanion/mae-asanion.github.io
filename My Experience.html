My Experience in Junior Highschool



Studying can be a lot of stressful there are times you will be up all night till morning just to finish your school assignments and or task, but, it can also be so much fun like having friends and by friends I mean a lot of friends also it might develope your social interaction skills without you even realizing it, if you just know how to balance both of them and set your priorities.

As of the moment I am a 2nd year college student and I could say that I had a lot of experience being a student. There was a time when I was in junior high school that I failed three of my subjects because I focused all of my attention and energy on having fun and it severely affected my grades. I think that was the time when I learned my lesson and eventually understand the importance of balance and knowing my priorities.

After that incident I am proud to say that never again in my life that I had experience it again. Maybe because I'm a bit older now more responsible of my actions. I hope that story of mine was able to enlighten you atleas the very little bit of what might happen if you haven't yet set your priorities and goals.
