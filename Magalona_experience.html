Be a Responsible and Manage your time with studies



I am currently a second-year college student, and I can say that I have a lot of academic experience. When I was in junior high and first-year college, I failed 2 subjects in Junior High and 1 subject in 1st-year college because I focused all of my energy and time on having fun playing games and spending time with friends, and it had a negative impact on my grades and I eventually learned my lesson.

I realized that making a list of everything you need to do is the best way to improve your time management. This may seem obvious, but in my experience, I leave important tasks until the last minute. Since that, I have encountered a failed grade so I created a timetable or a calendar on my phone. These tools help me.



Article written by Fiah Godery M. Magalona
