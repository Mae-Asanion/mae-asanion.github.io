How to manage your time as a Student



  • Plan ahead - Always make a schedule of the things you need to accomplish for the week and update it accordingly.

  • Don’t multitask - Break up your workload into manageable intervals. Work when you’re not focused is going to be sloppy. Refer to your schedule and act accordingly.

  • Set up your virtual office - Be as comfortable as you need to be, for you to be most productive. This might mean putting on a nice playlist, or being in complete silence.

  • Block out distractions - Try to eliminate distractions that may result in you neglecting your work, especially if it’s crucial. There are many ways to do this, have your phone away from you, have an app that locks away other apps and etc.

  • Reward yourself - Try incentivising yourself for every finished task you’ve accomplished. This could come in the form of a few minutes of break time or a snack.

  • Create a balance - Try to have a balanced schedule between your work and time as a student and your free time where you can take care of yourself and do whatever you want to do for leisure.

  • Get a good night’s sleep - We know considering the tagline of this blog that this advice coming from us is a bit ironic, but it isn’t that good to burn the midnight oils studying. Get adequate rest and take care of yourself as much as you can
